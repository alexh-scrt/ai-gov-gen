Everything Your Team Needs

AI Gov Gen handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on building responsible AI systems.

Multi-Category Risk Assessment

Structured questionnaire covering data governance, model risk, operational security, and compliance — aligned to NIST AI RMF and HKS AI Risk Framework.

Automated Risk Scoring

Weighted scoring engine maps responses to Low / Medium / High / Critical risk levels per category, with an overall AI deployment risk posture score out of 100.

Governance Checklist

Actionable control checklist generated from your assessment gaps, ordered by severity and mapped to framework control references.

SSP Control Entries

System Security Plan entries with implementation status, assessment findings, responsible roles, and remediation actions — ready for insertion into your existing SSP.

AI Use Policy Document

Full 11-section AI use policy populated with your system details, risk findings, and remediation priorities — formatted and ready for review.

DOCX & PDF Export

One-click download of all governance artifacts as professionally formatted DOCX (editable) and PDF (print-ready) files, with cover page, tables, and signature blocks.
